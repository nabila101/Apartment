Our Facilities





Luxury Pool

Dive into a world of tranquility at our state-of-the-art luxury pool. Surrounded by elegant landscaping and designed with premium finishes, our infinity-edge pool offers a serene escape from the bustling city. Enjoy private cabanas, plush loungers, and a dedicated sun deck perfect for unwinding or socializing. Whether you’re taking a refreshing swim, basking in the sun, or simply admiring the panoramic views, our pool facilities redefine leisure and sophistication.



High Standart Sport Center

Stay healthy and fit at our fitness center, equipped with modern equipment and comprehensive facilities. Our team of experienced instructors is ready to help you achieve your fitness goals. With tailored training programs, this fitness center becomes your hub for health and wellness transformation.



Five Stars Restaurants and Cafe

Discover the epitome of culinary excellence at our five-star restaurant and café. Indulge in a diverse menu crafted by expert chefs, featuring gourmet dishes made from the finest ingredients. Whether it’s a leisurely breakfast, a business lunch, or an elegant dinner, every meal is an unforgettable experience.



Green Outdoor Park

Step into a serene oasis at our beautifully landscaped green outdoor park, designed for relaxation and rejuvenation. Featuring lush greenery, walking paths, and tranquil seating areas, it’s the perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Whether you’re enjoying a peaceful stroll or a moment of reflection, this verdant space brings nature’s charm to your doorstep.



High Security

Skylight Apartment prioritizes the safety of its residents. Therefore, we provide a state-of-the-art security system that operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. The system includes CCTV monitoring throughout the complex, ensuring every corner and area is well maintained. With this, any activity that takes place around the property can be monitored live, providing a high level of security and providing peace of mind to all residents.
